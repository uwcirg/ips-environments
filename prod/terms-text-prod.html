Terms of Use

My Health Summary is a system intended to let you gather, organize, and share your health information from health care providers, government sources, and information you may wish to contribute to your record. You might want to do this when you are getting care from different providers who may not share electronic health records, if you need to verify immunizations or summarize traveler health information, if you need urgent or emergency care, or if you wishto share your health history with family, caregivers, or others.

In simple terms: we treat your health information as yours. We don’t sell it or intentionally share it. We use it only to run the system. While we take security seriously, you should decide carefully what you choose to upload. You can delete the information you upload, but we have not implemented the complete ability for you to specify that your use of the system “be forgotten”, and we may not be able to do so for reasons of security and data integrity.
